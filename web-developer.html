Web Developer

Mountain View, CA

Bring your creative and web development savviness to transform how enterprises innovate the next wave of applications and services. Build exciting and effective web presence and marketing to drive awareness and customer adoption of ElasticBox. If you are self-starter who thrives in a fast-paced, innovative culture, then, this role will unleash your potential. And you will have fun at it!

Responsibilities:

  • Lead the web development, content management, and site maintenance for a vibrant and engaging company website.
  • Integrate content such as documents, blogs, videos, and marketing collateral into the website in a timely manner to reflect our brand, value proposition, and messaging.
  • Design the creative placement and layout of content based on team inputs.
  • Gather specifications and requirements to plan and evolve our website.
  • Integrate best practices for SEO/SEM to drive traffic to the website.

Job Requirements:

  • 4+ years overall web development experience with expertise in HTML, CSS, PHP, Java Script, WordPress, and other web technologies.
  • Strong web portfolio that demonstrates integration of technology, design, and creativity to deliver a compelling user experience for any screen.
  • Demonstrated attention to detail even under fast-moving project needs and deadlines.
  • Strong communication and organization skills to balance numerous tasks, prioritize them, and get things done!
  • Understands and exploits integrated social media marketing through blogging, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, and other avenues.
  • Solid team player. Willing to listen but bold enough to challenge status quo.
  • Experience with database, content management systems, and developing the front-end for mobile applications a big plus.
Bonus points for:
  • Passion for web applications and technologies.
  • Bachelor's degree in graphics design or fine arts desired.
