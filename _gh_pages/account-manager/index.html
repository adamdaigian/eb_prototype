Account Manager

Mountain View, CA

Reporting directly to the Director of Sales, as Account Manager, you will be responsible for developing pipeline, creating campaigns, and driving sales. This role is a great opportunity to showcase your sales planning, execution, and customer engagement skills in the exciting area of cloud infrastructure and applications that are transforming how enterprises innovate and create differentiation.

Responsibilities:

  • Meet and exceed revenue targets for the region
  • Collaborate with Sales Director on annual business plan and provide quarterly forecast updates
  • Understand market trends and their impact on various verticals and develop customer engagement strategy
  • Campaign and prospect to drive new incremental opportunities and pipeline
  • Qualify inbound leads and compile target lists for campaigns and outbound calling
  • Manage and document the details of the sales cycle in Salesforce.com and extract insights
  • Drive net new customers as well as grow install base accounts with existing product offerings.
  • Develop cross-selling strategies, best practices, and reference accounts for various customer segments.
  • Effectively articulate the value of software to both technical and non-technical buyers
  • Establish winning partnerships with channel partners and system integrators

Job Requirements

Must Have:
  • #1 Top Gun Ranking and Over-Achievement of goals
  • Thorough understanding of salesprocess techniques and strategies
  • Application managemnet or related IT specific software sales experience preferred
  • 3+ years of experience in technology infrastructure/application sales targeting enterprise customers
  • Strategic Sales Training/Completed Courses
  • Experience turning a technical sales into a ROI/TCO business analysis and sale
