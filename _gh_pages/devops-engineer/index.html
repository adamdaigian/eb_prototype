DevOps Engineer

Mountain View, CA

How would you like to work for a company that's revolutionizing the world of SaaS and PaaS? ElasticBox is redefining how developers and enterprises deploy applications to the cloud. We empower them to deploy and manage the most complex applications and topologies in just a few clicks.

As part of the DevOps team, you'll be responsible to shape how enterprises and developers combine the latest technologies and services to automate all aspects of software deployment and instance lifecycle management. You must have a passion for software automation and quality!

Responsibilities:

  • Apply a thorough understanding of deploying and managing application lifecycles to interpret customer business needs and processes.
  • Work with the inside sales team to give technical demos and proof-of-concepts.
  • Work with the development team to improve the system architecture.
  • Help, manage, and create sample deployments and customer best practices.
  • Collaborate with other developers, testers, and system engineers to ensure quality product enhancements.

Job Requirements

Must Have:
  • 3+ years of scripting language development experience.
  • 3+ years of experience working in a Linux/Unix development environment.
  • Experience implementing High Availability and Disaster Recovery architectures.
  • Experience deploying and managing production systems on a cloud infrastructure
  • Detailed understanding of networking at all layers.
  • Experience with good engineering practices (test driven development, continuous integration, etc.)
  • Ability (and enthusiasm) in mentoring less experienced DevOps team members..
The preferred candidate has:
  • A degree in computer science or information systems.
  • In-depth knowledge of cloud and virtualization technologies like Amazon Web Services, VMware cloud technology, and OpenStack.
  • Experience developing and supporting cloud infrastructure designs and implementations.
  • Experience supporting always-available SaaS applications.
  • Experience using DevOps frameworks like Puppet and Chef
  • Experience integrating distributed systems through Web based APIs.
  • Familiar with concepts related to continuous integration and continuous deployment.
