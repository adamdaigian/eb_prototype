ElasticBox Access Control

Top level policy control, oversight and transparency.

Maintaining governance, policy and strategy at the same time is hard in a CIO, CTO or IT Ops role. ElasticBox gives you the centralized oversight needed, with high level transparency and granular detail, to keep strategy in place and your team focused on innovating.
  • Independent users, unified control and strategy.
  • Strategic usage of provider resources and IT assets.
  • Universal admin control panel to manage users and environments.
  • Define policy and implementation standards.
  • History of user and resource access.

Workspaces Organize Infrastructure

Organize and isolate applications and environments.

ElasticBox workspaces all you and your team to organize and isolate applications, providers, users, variables and anything else you can imagine. Keep your development, test and production environments separate so they can run at full speed. Distribute resources by workspaces and get detailed reports on who does what.
  • Isolate applications and services across environments.
  • Drop in policies for environments and their users.
  • Seamlessly migrate between environments without changing users or roles.
  • Manage assets, permissions and dependencies on an environment level.
  • Gain control and visibility over environment specific operations.
