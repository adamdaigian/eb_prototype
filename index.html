Build applications your way.

ElasticBox is a simplified and modular application development platform, with fully configured components of your application architecture encapsulated as “Boxes” and made available to you as a service.





Drive innovation with agility.

1) Select your Boxes, 2) Stack Boxes to create the application architecture, and 3) Deploy the app. Not only are boxes standalone and contained, they are also reusable and can be versioned and updated, fully mobile across all your cloud environments.
