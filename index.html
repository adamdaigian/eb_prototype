What makes ElasticBox special?

Our integrated platform to develop and deploy applications faster. Manage all your virtual infrastructure in one place like never before.

Build applications your way.

ElasticBox is a simplified, modular application development platform, with fully configured components of your application architecture encapsulated as “Boxes” and made available to you as a service.





Drive innovation with agility?

Select and stack Boxes to create your application architecture. Then deploy in a single click. Not only are boxes standalone and contained, they are also reusable, versionablem can be updated easily and are fully mobile across all your cloud environments.
